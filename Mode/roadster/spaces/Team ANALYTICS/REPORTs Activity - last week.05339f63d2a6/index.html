Loyal User per Report: If a report has at least 1 loyal user, it's valuable to someone. If there aren't any loyal users then we should improve it, promote it or delete it. We chose to consider someone loyal to a report if they pull that report up on 3 distinct days in the past 6 weeks (every other week). This includes Roadster Employees - if a report demos well, we consider it valuable.


Super Users: Super User are users who are our heaviest report users. We chose a panel of users using the criteria they must have interacted with the reports in at least 6 of the past 13 weeks... and an interaction must be more than 2 reports-days (2 different reports in a single day or the same report in at least 2 distinct days).
